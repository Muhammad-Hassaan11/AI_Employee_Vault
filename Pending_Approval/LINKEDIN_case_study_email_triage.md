Case study: we pointed an AI Employee at a drowning inbox. Here's what actually happened.

The problem: hundreds of emails a week — real client messages buried under notifications, promotions, and follow-ups that kept slipping. Triage alone was eating more than an hour a day.

The setup: an AI Employee that watches the inbox around the clock. Every incoming email becomes an organized task. Noise gets filed. Real messages get a drafted reply. Nothing is ever sent without a human clicking approve.

The results after the first weeks:

- Every client email triaged and turned into a task automatically, 24/7
- Replies arrive pre-drafted — review and approve instead of write from scratch
- Follow-ups tracked in one dashboard, so nothing slips through the cracks
- A full log of every action, so there's always an answer to "what happened with that email?"

The owner's job changed from "dig through the inbox" to "approve the good drafts." That's the whole point: AI does the shuffling, the human keeps the judgment.
