Every email, post, and payment our AI Employee drafts goes through one non-negotiable step: a human approval before it goes anywhere.

Not because the AI can't do it — because trust in automation is built one correct decision at a time, and the fastest way to lose that trust is a system that acts without you.

Human-in-the-loop isn't a limitation. It's the feature that lets you actually sleep at night while it works.

That's the whole philosophy behind how we build these systems.
