The real cost of manual admin work isn't the 20 minutes it takes today — it's the 20 minutes, every working day, for the rest of the year. That adds up to roughly 80+ hours spent on tasks a system could have handled before you finished your morning coffee.

Automation isn't about replacing effort. It's about spending your hours on the parts of the business only you can actually do.

Worth an honest audit of where your week goes — happy to help you run one if you want a second pair of eyes.
