A day in the life of an AI Employee:

🌅 Morning — pulls new emails and WhatsApp messages, sorts urgent from routine
📝 Midday — drafts replies, follow-ups, and social captions in your voice
✅ Afternoon — queues anything sensitive (sends, posts, payments) for your approval
🌙 Evening — logs everything it did, updates the dashboard, ready for tomorrow

You stay the decision-maker. It just handles everything leading up to the decision.
