A question we get a lot: "isn't an AI agent just automation with a new name?"

Not quite. Traditional automation follows a fixed rule: if X happens, do Y, every time. An AI agent reasons through the situation first — it reads the context, decides what the right response is, and only then acts (asking you to approve anything sensitive along the way).
