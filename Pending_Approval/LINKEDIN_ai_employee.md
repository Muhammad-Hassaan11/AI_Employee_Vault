What if your business had an employee who never sleeps, never forgets a follow-up, and costs less than a part-time hire?

That's what an AI Employee is. Not a chatbot — a working system that plugs into your business and quietly handles the repetitive work:

- Reads and triages your email inbox, drafting replies for your approval
- Keeps your books current in your accounting system
- Drafts your LinkedIn and social content on a schedule
- Logs everything it does, so you can audit every action

The key detail most people miss: a good AI Employee never acts alone on anything sensitive. Payments, sends, and posts all wait for a human yes. You stay in control — you just stop doing the busywork.
