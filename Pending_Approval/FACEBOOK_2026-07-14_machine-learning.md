What actually is "machine learning"? 🤖

Simple version: it's software that learns from examples instead of following rules someone typed out by hand.

Show it a thousand emails you marked "important," and it learns to spot the next important one on its own. Show it your past sales, and it starts predicting which new leads are worth your time.

The best part for a small business? You don't need a huge budget or a tech team to benefit from it. You just need one repetitive task that eats your day — and a system smart enough to learn it, with you approving the important calls.

That's exactly what we build: AI that learns your business and quietly does the busywork, while you stay in control.
