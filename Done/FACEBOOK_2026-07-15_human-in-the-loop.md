Not because the AI can't handle it — but because trust in automation gets built one correct decision at a time, and the fastest way to lose that trust is a system that acts without you.

Human-in-the-loop isn't a limitation we're working around. It's the actual design — the part that lets you hand off the busywork and still sleep at night knowing nothing moves without your sign-off.
